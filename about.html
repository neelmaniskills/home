About Neelmani Skills

Empowering youth with job-ready skills for immediate employment

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Neelmani Skills is a initiative of Neelmani EduTech India Pvt Ltd, dedicated to bridging the gap between unemployment and job opportunities through short-term skill development programs.

Our model is inspired by the successful PMKVY (Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana) program, focusing on providing practical training in fields that require minimal training but offer steady income opportunities.

We specialize in courses like tailoring, basic computer skills, healthcare assistance, and other vocational skills that can help students start earning quickly.

Our Director

Leading the vision of skill development

Our Approach

We focus on:

  • Short duration courses (1-3 months) for quick employability
  • Practical hands-on training rather than theoretical knowledge
  • Industry-relevant skills that are in demand locally
  • Placement assistance to connect students with employers
  • Affordable fees with easy payment options

Our training centers are equipped with modern tools and equipment to provide realistic work environment experience.
